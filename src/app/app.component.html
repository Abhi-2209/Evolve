HEALTH ASSESEMENT & FITNESS TESTING

Before you can start a training and nutritional plan, or at least one that's actually going to be effective, I will carry out a number of tests to establish your current state of health and fitness. You see there isn't a blueprint for weight management.

How you need to train and what you need to eat is different for everyone, even if the goal is the same. It's going to depend on your age, gender, somatotype (body type) and lifestyle. So...how can someone possibly give you a bespoke nutritional and exercise programme (not a useless general plan) if they don't know anything about your body? How can you track the effects of your training and diet if you don't have a benchmark? Here is what I will aim to find out about you:

Resting Heart Rate

Resting Blood Pressure

Height/Weight & Body Composition (Body fat/Skeletal muscle/Water)

Visceral Fat BMR (Basic Metabolic Rate)

Cardiorespiratory fitness & Cardiorespiratory recovery rate

Muscular Strength & Muscular Endurance

Flexibility Posture

LIFESTYLE ASSESEMENT AND PLAN

Did you know that 80% of your weight loss or weight management results come from the time you spend out of the gym? ! Lets say you train at the gym 4 times a week for 1 hour at a time, that amounts to less than 2% of your week. Therefore, if you don't have a suitable plan for your time outside the gym then you are severely compromising your results. I go beyond the gym walls and ensure you are living a lifestyle that will help to get you into shape and feeling great!
